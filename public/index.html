Our Story!

We are a young Delhi-based boutique studio

So, now that you’ve decided to get married (tadaaaa), allow us to tell you a little-something about ourselves. We plan your “d-day d way you like it”.

Our name Vivah La Vida, is inspired by a Spanish phrase called “Viva La Vida” which means “Long, Live, Life” (Coldplay fans, we hear you!)

A wedding is a moment that should indeed be full of life. This phrase fuels our philosophy, cultivates passion and inspires us everyday to keep on keeping on.

We understand how delicate, emotional, stressful, and overwhelmingly happy a wedding can be. Hence, we follow a personal approach to understand your liking, your vision of how you want your day to be, and accordingly design a wedding of your dreams. Let’s picture it together, shall we?
